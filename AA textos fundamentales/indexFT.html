Quick hummus recipe

This recipe makes hummus fast and tasty, without getting dirty.      It has adapted from several different recipes that I have read over the years.      Hummus is a delicious thick paste that is widely used in Greek and Middle Eastern dishes.      It is very tasty with salad, grilled meats and pitta breads.

    Ingredients
            
  • 1 can (400 g) of chickpeas.
    •         
  • 175 g of tahini.
    •         
  • 6 dried tomatoes.
    •         
  • Half red pepper.
    •         
  • A pinch of cayenne pepper.
    •         
  • 1 clove of garlic.
    •         
  • A pinch of olive oil.
    Instructions
            
  1. Remove garlic skin and chop into large pieces.
    2.         
  2. Remove all the seeds and stem of the pepper, and chop into large pieces.
    3.         
  3. Add all the ingredients in a food processor.
    4.         
  4. Process all ingredients in a paste.
    5.         
                  
                  
    • If you want a thick hummus, process it for a short time.
      •             
                  
    • If you want a soft hummus, process it for longer.
    To get a different flavor
    you can mix a small amount of;
  • lemon and cilantro.
  • or chili pepper.
  • or lime and chipotle.
  • or harissa and mint.
  • or spinach and feta cheese.

Experiment and see what works for you.

Storage
Refrigerate the finished hummus in a sealed container. I should be able to use it during About a week after doing so. If it starts to bubble, definitely you must discard it.
Hummus is suitable for freezing;you must defrost it and use it within a couple of months.
